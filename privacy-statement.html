Privacy Statement for Education/Lunchroom

Games4U has been committed to giving a way for kids to talk and have fun!

Now, more than ever, kids need a way to socialize with each other during this pandemic. Games4U is offering this by allowing you to connect your device to Azure servers to be managed by Games4U. This allows for children to use the Lunchroom service and other education services provided by Games4U, LLC. However, by linking your devices to our servers, you give your consent to:

  • Add a shortcut on your device for Lunchroom and, if in Palm Beach County Schools, provide links for SDPBC websites.
  • To monitor device for any misuse of Games4U services
  • To allow for shared user login between anyone that has a Azure AD account with Games4U

We are commited to your privacy, and will not

  • Track your device's location
  • Track browsing history
  • Place cookies on browser to track user (as that is illegal)
  • Open Camera without consent of user
  • And any other privacy invading measure there is.
